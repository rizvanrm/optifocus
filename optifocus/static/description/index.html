Overview

The Odoo Optifocus Module is a comprehensive solution tailored for optical clinics, retailers, and wholesalers. It streamlines operations across multiple departments, ensuring efficiency and accuracy in managing sales, inventory, purchases, accounting, and insurance. The module is packed with specialized features designed to cater to the unique needs of the optical industry.

Sales Management

  • Supports three types of sales:
    • Retail: For direct customers.
    • Insurance: Integrated with insurance policies and claims.
    • Wholesale: For bulk orders and B2B transactions.
  • Facilitates E-invoicing for compliance.
  • Tracks payments and generates detailed sales reports.
  • Commercial partners feature, allows customers to shop now and pay later in installments.
  • Workflow for Insurance Orders, ensuring smooth processing through distinct stages: Draft, Approved, Rejected, and Reverted
  • Undelivered Order Reports feature provides businesses with insights into orders that have been partially delivered or remain undelivered.

Inventory Management

  • Product Categorization: Classify products into categories like frames, sunglasses, power lens, sun lens, contact lenses, and optical accessories.
  • Advanced Product Attributes: Define product specifications, including:
    • Frame - Frame Color, Size
    • Sunglass - Frame Color, Lens Color, Lens Material, Size.
    • Power Lens - Lens Material, Lens Index, Focal, Coating, Photochromic, Polarized, Sphere, Cylinder, Addition,Axis, Diameter, Base Curve.
    • Sun Lens - Lens Material, Diameter, Base Curve.
    • Contact Lens - Lens Color, Sphere, Diameter, Base Curve.
  • Define Brands and their Models
  • Stock Transfers: Seamless stock transfer between branches.
  • Workflow Management: For job orders related to Lens fitting or custom products.
  • Inventory Adjustments: Easily manage inventory discrepancies.
  • Scrap Management: Track and dispose of defective products.
  • Print barcode labels for streamlined product tracking.
  • Automated Purchase Orders for RX Lenses.
  • Stock and Inventory Adjustment Reports in PDF and Excel Format.

Purchase Management

  • Vendor Management.
  • Purchase Orders.
  • Receive Products.
  • Vendor Invoice.
  • Reporting and Analytics.
